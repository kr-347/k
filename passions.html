Video Editing

I like video editing because it turns scattered clips, ideas, and moments into something organized and meaningful. A good edit is not just effects; it is pacing, clarity, emotion, and knowing what to cut.

What I enjoy: building a story, matching visuals with sound, improving transitions, and making the final product feel clean.

Forensics and Mystery Solving

I’m interested in forensics because it feels like solving a mystery with science. Details like fingerprints, fibers, patterns, chromatography, or material tests can help explain what happened.

What I enjoy: observing carefully, comparing evidence, staying logical, and building conclusions from clues instead of guesses.

Coding and Robotics

Coding and robotics are exciting because they make problem-solving visible. When something does not work, I can test, debug, redesign, and improve it step by step.
